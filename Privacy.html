Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 3/19/2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Altairly ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects user data when you use our Chrome extension.

By using our extension, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Our Chrome extension collects the following data to provide its functionality:

  • Cart Total Information: We collect your cart total amount when you visit certain Amazon pages (e.g., /cart, /gp/cart, and /gp/buy/) to display relevant savings and deals.
  • User Activity: We may track user interactions with the extension, including clicks and whether the popup is opened.
  • Website Content: We access page elements (e.g., cart total text) on supported Amazon pages to retrieve cart total amounts.

We do not collect any of the following:

  • Personally Identifiable Information (PII), such as your name, email address, or payment details.
  • Health information, location data, or authentication credentials.
  • Financial or payment information.
  • Web history beyond the Amazon cart and checkout pages.

2. How We Use Collected Data

  • Calculate and display your current Amazon cart total.
  • Determine and highlight potential savings.
  • Re-open the extension popup when the cart total changes.

3. Data Sharing and Security

  • No Data Sharing: We do not sell, share, or transfer user data to third parties for marketing or unrelated purposes.
  • Data Security: Any data temporarily stored (e.g., cart total) is handled securely and is used only to perform the extension's intended functionality.
  • No Third-Party Tracking: We do not use third-party analytics or tracking services.

4. Remote Code Usage

Our extension does not use or load remote code. All functionality is handled locally by the extension scripts installed in your Chrome browser.

5. Permissions Explanation

  • activeTab: Used to interact with the current Amazon tab when the user is on relevant cart or checkout pages.
  • scripting: Used to execute scripts on Amazon cart and checkout pages to extract cart total data.
  • storage: Used to temporarily store the cart total value locally.
  • Host Permissions (e.g., https://www.amazon.com/*): Required to access and extract data from supported Amazon pages where the cart total is displayed.

6. User Rights

  • Access: Request the data collected by the extension.
  • Delete Data: Clear your cart total information from local storage by disabling or uninstalling the extension.
  • Opt-out: You can disable the extension at any time in Chrome.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.

Why Pin the Extension?

Pinning the extension to your Chrome toolbar ensures quick access when you need to check your cart total and potential savings on Amazon. Click the puzzle icon (⚙️) in Chrome, and click the pin icon next to Roundup to Free Shipping.
